Lecture 03:


A hyperplane in \( \mathbb{R}^d \) is represented by the equation:

\[
\mathbf{w} \cdot \mathbf{x} + b = 0
\]

where \( \mathbf{w} \) is the normal vector, and \( b \) is the threshold.
